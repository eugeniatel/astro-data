Create src/render/ directory and src/render/wheel.ts.

The renderer works in these steps:
1. Create a blank character grid (rows x cols)
2. Draw the outer circle (zodiac ring)
3. Place zodiac sign abbreviations on the outer ring at their midpoints
4. Draw the inner circle (house ring)
5. Draw house cusp lines radiating from inner circle to outer circle
6. Place house numbers near each cusp
7. Place planet glyphs between inner and outer rings at their ecliptic positions
8. Return the grid as string[][] and a helper to convert to string

Key math:
- eclipticToScreenAngle(longitude, ascendant): maps ecliptic longitude to screen angle where ASC is at PI (9 o'clock). Screen angle goes counter-clockwise from 0=right.
- Formula: screenAngle = PI + (ascendant - longitude) * PI / 180
  (Subtracting because zodiac goes counter-clockwise on the wheel)
- polarToGrid(angle, radius, centerRow, centerCol, aspectRatio): converts polar to grid position with aspect ratio correction on the x-axis
